# Decision Tree for Daily Reflection Agent

## Root Question (Q1)
**Did you complete your main goal today?**

### ├── YES
│   └── Q2: How did you feel?
│       ├── Productive
│       │   └── Output A: Insight – You turn intentions into action. 
│       │       Action – Keep a “win log” daily to build momentum.
│       │
│       ├── Neutral
│       │   └── Output B: Insight – Completion without satisfaction often means low alignment. 
│       │       Action – Tomorrow, rate goal importance (1–5) before starting.
│       │
│       └── Stressed
│           └── Output C: Insight – You achieved the goal but depleted energy. 
│               Action – Break next goal into 3 smaller steps + schedule a break.

### └── NO
    └── Q2: Why not?
        ├── Lack of time
        │   └── Output F: Insight – Time may be leaking due to poor estimation or interruptions. 
        │       Action – Track time in 30-min blocks for one day.
        │
        ├── Distraction
        │   └── Q3: Type of distraction?
        │       ├── Phone
        │       │   └── Output D: Insight – Your phone becomes a high-frequency interruptor. 
        │       │       Action – Use “focus mode” 25 min before touching your main goal.
        │       │
        │       ├── Social Media
        │       │   └── Output E: Insight – Social media pulls you into reactive consumption. 
        │       │       Action – Log out of accounts until goal is done.
        │       │
        │       └── Environment (people, noise, etc.)
        │           └── Output H: Insight – Your environment is not optimized for deep work. 
        │               Action – Create a 2-hour “do not disturb” physical setup.
        │
        ├── Low motivation
        │   └── Q3: Why low motivation?
        │       ├── Burnout
        │       │   └── Output I: Insight – You might be overloading without recovery. 
        │       │       Action – Take a real 30-min rest before deciding next task.
        │       │
        │       ├── No clarity
        │       │   └── Output J: Insight – Vague goals kill drive. 
        │       │       Action – Rewrite goal as “I will [specific action] at [specific time]”.
        │       │
        │       └── Task too hard
        │           └── Output K: Insight – Difficulty without scaffolding leads to avoidance. 
        │               Action – Reduce scope to 5% version (e.g., write 1 sentence, open 1 file).
        │
        └── Unclear goal
            └── Output L: Insight – A fuzzy goal guarantees uncertain execution. 
                Action – Tomorrow: define “done” in one measurable sentence before starting.
